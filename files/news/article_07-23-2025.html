Wayfresh: The New Shopping Simulation from Laius Entertainment Launching Next Month!

Igor Kalen

Get ready to dive into the world of retail with Wayfresh1, Laius Entertainment’s newest all-ages2 shopping simulation game! Whether you want to be a cashier, manage the store, keep security tight, or explore other cool roles, Wayfresh gives you a realistic and fun experience of running a busy store from every angle.

The game focuses on teamwork, quick thinking, and customer service — but with a chill, family-friendly vibe perfect for players of all ages. From managing queues to handling unexpected events, every shift will keep you on your toes.
