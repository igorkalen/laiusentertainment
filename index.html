Information for Parents

What is Laius Entertainment?

Laius Entertainment creates educational ROBLOX experiences designed to teach valuable life skills through engaging gameplay. Our flagship experience, Ronald's Restaurant, simulates working at a fast-paced restaurant environment where children learn customer service, teamwork, reading, and communication skills in a safe, supervised environment.

Safety & Age Appropriateness

All our experiences are rated for all ages by ROBLOX. We maintain a dedicated Trust & Safety team working around the clock to ensure secure environments. Our experiences promote positive values like helping others, working together, and being responsible. We actively moderate content and maintain family-friendly environments with zero tolerance for inappropriate content or unsafe interactions.

Getting Started

If your child doesn't have a ROBLOX account yet, here's how to get started:

  1. Visit roblox.com and create a free account
  2. Set up parental controls and privacy settings (optional but highly recommended for children & teens)
  3. Download the ROBLOX app on your device
  4. Search for our experiences like Ronald's Restaurant and click Play
  5. Your child can start learning and having fun immediately!

Educational Benefits

Playing our experiences helps children develop:

  • Customer service and communication skills
  • Reading comprehension and basic math through interactive tasks
  • Teamwork and collaboration abilities
  • Responsibility and strong work ethic
  • Problem-solving and critical thinking skills
  • Social skills through positive peer interactions
  • Understanding of workplace dynamics and professional behavior
