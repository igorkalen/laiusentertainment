Laius Entertainment is a company creating educational games on the ROBLOX platform.

Ronald's Restaurant is a fast-food simulation game on ROBLOX inspired by the world’s biggest fast food chain. Players can join in as cooks, cashiers, janitors, customers, or managers in a friendly and educational environment.

The game teaches real-world skills like customer service, teamwork, reading, and communication while providing a fun experience for all ages.

Players can choose from multiple roles: cook, cashier, janitor, customer, or manager. Each role offers unique skills development.

The community is global and welcoming, helping new players learn quickly.

Ronald's Restaurant helps develop:

  • Customer service and communication skills
  • Reading and basic math skills through order taking
  • Teamwork and collaboration
  • Responsibility and work ethic
  • Problem-solving abilities
  • Positive social interactions

The game is rated for all ages by ROBLOX and includes a dedicated Trust & Safety team for moderation. It maintains a family-friendly, safe environment with no inappropriate content.

Parents are encouraged to monitor children as needed.

To start playing Ronald's Restaurant without a ROBLOX account:

  1. Visit roblox.com and create a free account.
  2. Set up parental controls and privacy settings (optional but recommended).
  3. Download the ROBLOX app on your device.
  4. Search for Ronald's Restaurant and click Play.
