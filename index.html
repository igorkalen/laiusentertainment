Information for Parents

What is Ronald's Restaurant?

Ronald's Restaurant is an educational ROBLOX experience that simulates working at McDonald's. Your child can learn valuable life skills like customer service, teamwork, reading, and communication in a safe, supervised environment. Ronald's Group is designed to be both fun and educational, making learning feel like play.

Safety & Age Appropriateness

Our experience is rated for all ages by ROBLOX. We have a dedicated Trust & Safety team working around the clock to keep things secure. We maintain a family-friendly environment with active moderation. Ronald's Group teaches positive values like helping others, working together, and being responsible. No inappropriate content or unsafe interactions are allowed.

Getting Started Without ROBLOX

If your child doesn't have a ROBLOX account yet, here's how to get started:

  1. Visit roblox.com and create a free account
  2. Set up parental controls and privacy settings (optional but highly recommended for children & teens)
  3. Download the ROBLOX app on your device
  4. Search for Ronald's Restaurant and click Play
  5. Your child can start learning and having fun immediately!

Educational Benefits

Playing Ronald's Restaurant helps children develop:

  • Customer service and communication skills
  • Reading and basic math through order taking
  • Teamwork and collaboration
  • Responsibility and work ethic
  • Problem-solving abilities
  • Social skills through positive interactions
